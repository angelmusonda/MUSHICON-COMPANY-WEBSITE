Design Services

1. Site Design

We create detailed site layouts for various infrastructure projects, including buildings, roads, and bridges. Our planning includes the arrangement of structures, parking lots, drainage systems, and landscaping to enhance both functionality and aesthetics.

2. Architectural Design

We produce custom architectural designs to meet the unique needs of our clients while complying with regulatory requirements. Using advanced technology, we generate 2D plans and 3D models to bring design concepts to life.

3. Structural Design

We design the structural components of buildings, bridges, and other structures to ensure resilience against loads and environmental conditions. Our approach involves using sophisticated software and accurate hand calculations to determine structural responses, adhering to industry codes for specifying structural layouts and member sizes.

4. Seismic Structural Design

We emphasise the importance of seismic design since seismicity in Zambia is not given the serious attention it deserves. Our team of experts designs structures capable of withstanding earthquakes, prioritising life safety through the application of the capacity design method.

5. Infrastructure Rehabilitation Design

We specialise in redesigning and rehabilitating ageing infrastructure or structures undergoing purpose transitions. Whether transforming residential spaces into commercial or preserving old structures, we conduct thorough structural assessments and provide retrofitting or strengthening solutions.

6. Geotechnical Design

We conduct in-depth analyses of soil and rock conditions to offer precise foundation recommendations. Our expertise extends to designing retaining walls, slope stabilisation measures, and other geotechnical structures for optimal project outcomes.

7. Transportation Design

We plan and design comprehensive transportation systems such as roads and highways. Our focus is on traffic optimisation, innovative intersection designs, and incorporating robust safety features.

8. Water Resources Design

We expertly design water supply and distribution systems, stormwater management, and flood control systems. Our solutions also include the design of wastewater treatment facilities for sustainable and efficient infrastructure.

9. Environmental Design

We create environmentally conscious designs for protection and remediation systems. Our commitment is to develop sustainable solutions that minimise the environmental impact of infrastructure projects.
