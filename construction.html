Construction Management

1. Project Planning:

Developing project objectives, scope, and constraints. Creating project schedules and timelines. Establishing a budget and financial plan. Conducting feasibility studies.

2. Design Management:

Coordinating with architects and engineers during the design phase. Reviewing and approving design documents. Ensuring compliance with building codes and regulations. Engaging in value engineering to optimise design and cost.

3. Contract Management:

Developing and negotiating contracts with contractors, suppliers, and subcontractors. Administering contracts and managing contract changes. Ensuring compliance with contractual obligations.

4. Cost Estimation and Budgeting:

Developing accurate cost estimates for the project. Monitoring and controlling project costs. Forecasting and managing budgetary aspects throughout the project.

5. Risk Management:

Identifying potential risks and developing risk mitigation strategies. Monitoring and managing risks throughout the project life cycle. Implementing contingency plans as needed.

6. Quality Control and Assurance:

Establishing quality standards and specifications. Conducting inspections and quality assurance checks. Implementing quality control measures to ensure compliance.

7. Safety Management:

Developing and enforcing safety protocols and procedures. Monitoring and promoting a safe working environment. Conducting safety training for workers and contractors.

8. Communication and Reporting:

Facilitating communication among project stakeholders. Providing regular progress reports to clients and stakeholders. Addressing and resolving issues as they arise.

9. Construction Site Management:

Supervising construction activities on-site. Ensuring adherence to project plans and specifications. Managing and coordinating subcontractors and suppliers.

10. Schedule Management:

Developing and maintaining project schedules. Monitoring progress and adjusting schedules as needed. Mitigating delays and optimising construction timelines.

11. Closeout and Handover:

Ensuring completion of all construction activities. Conducting inspections and quality checks for final acceptance. Managing the handover of the project to the client.
