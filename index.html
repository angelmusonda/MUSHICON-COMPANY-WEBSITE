Who We Are

We are a leading civil engineering consulting firm based in Mukulumpe, Kasama,Zambia. With a strong commitment to excellence and innovation, we specialize in providing a wide range of services to meet the unique needs of our clients. Our services are tailored to meet the needs of small infrastructure developers while maintaining the capacity to deliver on large-scale projects.

Our mission is to contribute to the development of robust and efficient infrastructure that positively impacts communities. With a team of certified and experienced engineers, Mushicon is your reliable partner for all civil engineering projects.

Our Services

Project Planning and Feasibility Studies

We systematically evaluate and plan construction or infrastructure projects. This includes outlining project objectives, scheduling, and resource allocation. Our feasibility studies assess factors like environmental impact, regulatory compliance, and economic viability, providing insights for clients to make informed decisions about project viability. This diligence minimises risks and ensures a well-organised foundation for subsequent project stages.

Design Works

We provide a wide range of design services such as architectural and structural designs. We design transportation systems and water treatment/distribution systems. Our focus is on using modern software solutions to optimise designs while adhering to regulatory frameworks. Driven by a commitment to excellence, we ensure that our designs blend innovation with practicality. Our goal is to deliver solutions that not only meet client expectations but also contribute to sustainable and compliant outcomes.

Construction Management

Our construction management serivices include the ongoing process of planning, coordinating, and supervising projects from the start to the end. The key activities we perform include developing project objectives, schedules, and budgets; negotiating and administering contracts; estimating and controlling costs; identifying and mitigating risks; ensuring quality and safety standards; and supervising on-site activities.
